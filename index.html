What is This?

Achan-Detector is a simple tool that notifies you when your dad leaves to pick you up. No more need to call and ask "where are you?"—this portal gives you a live ETA.

How It Works

The system uses a live video feed and location tracking via a secure API. When your dad leaves a designated area, the status updates automatically and calculates the estimated time of arrival.
